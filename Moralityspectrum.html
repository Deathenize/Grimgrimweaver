Pure Good

Selflessly devoted to helping others, always chooses
the right path regardless of difficulty.

Gray Good

Generally good but willing to bend the rules or
make tough moral compromises for the greater good.

True Neutral

Balanced, acts based on logic,
self-interest, or circumstance rather than morality.

Gray Evil

Selfish or ruthless but not entirely without
redeeming qualities; may justify harm for personal goals.

Pure Evil

A lack of redeeming qualities, complete cruelty,
and joy in suffering, driven by hatred, sadism, or a thirst for destruction.
