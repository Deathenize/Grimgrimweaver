｡°✩★ Name: Nine (The small intestine)


A big fan of small snacks and tasty drinks, Nine is the youngest of the three Digestion brothers in the Organ Department. Unlike the others, he never kills blood cells—actually, he loves lifting them up and giving them treats instead. He’s sweet, playful, and full of energy but also incredibly passive, making him an easy target for bullying. Often lost in his own little world, Nine can be pretty aloof and unreactive, rarely standing up for himself. Half of his face is always covered, but according to Venture, he has distractingly beautiful eyes—not that it makes up for how stupid he is. Still, despite his lack of brains, he’s bubbly, friendly, and always up for a snack break.
He can also bake very well!
